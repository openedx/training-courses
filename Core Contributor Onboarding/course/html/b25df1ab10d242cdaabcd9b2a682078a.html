When you're deciding how to use your contributor hours, there's lots to choose from. Consider some of the following tasks:

  • Pull request, ADR, & OEP reviews (note: as a CC you are by default a core contributor to the open-edx-proposals repo. Make your opinions known!)
  • Working on new features (see the Open edX roadmap for inspiration)
  • Responding to the community in the forums or Slack - which may uncover some interesting to-do items not yet noted before
  • Working group attendance, participation, and execution on follow-up work
  • Cleaning up documentation (on Confluence, in the docs.openedx.org repo, or within your repos)
  • Looking at Sprint Retrospective threads in the Core Contributors forum space to see if anyone else is looking for help you can provide
