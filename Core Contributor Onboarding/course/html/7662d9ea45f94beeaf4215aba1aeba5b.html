Core Contributors are pillars of our community. You've become a Core Contributor by demonstrating the "3 C's":

  • Commitment - you are able to spend focused time on the project on a regular basis, shown over a large period of time;
  • Conduct - Your conduct is consistent with the Open edX Code of Conduct. You are open, friendly, and welcoming. When you receive constructive feedback, you listen to it and take it as a learning opportunity and a way to level up. You help and mentor junior members of the community.
  • Caliber - You contributions are ones that consistently meet the bar of excellence in your given area.

A fourth "C" might stand for Collaborative leadership. As a Core Contributor, we expect you to practice collaborative leadership, which is exemplified by working together, attending working groups, and/or sharing your work on Slack, forums, GitHub, Confluence, or other places. Collaborative leaders take feedback and direction from others; they do not unilaterally rule a team or project.
