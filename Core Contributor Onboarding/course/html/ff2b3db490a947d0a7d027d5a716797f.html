The Core Contributor community is friendly, welcoming, and cooperative. We aim to sync our work via two-week retrospectives and do a read out every two weeks in the Contributor's Meetup to discuss ways to improve. As part of the retrospective we ask people to report their number of hours spent doing CC work so we can monitor the success of the program; we'll discuss this more in the coming sections.

Start getting involved today! Here's some things we'd love for you to do:

  • Say Hello to everyone in the #core-contributors Slack channel
  • Join the Discussion Forums and participate, particularly answering questions in areas you're an expert in
  • Make sure your information is accurate on the Core Contributor wiki page
  • Consider joining the next Contributor's Meetup to get a sense for what it is (every other Tuesday; see the Working Groups shared calendar)
  • Consider joining other Working Groups, depending on your areas of expertise
