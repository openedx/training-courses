As a Core Contributor to one or more repos, you now have an additional responsibility towards the repo's health. This includes things like dependency upgrades, security fixes, and reviewing OSPRs. If you're not a maintainer in your repo, you are not directly responsible for these things; however, as part of your 20h/month commitment we encourage you to consider making the health of your repo a priority for at least some of those hours.
