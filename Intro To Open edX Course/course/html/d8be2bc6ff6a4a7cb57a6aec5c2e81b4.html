Who Defines the Roadmap?

The roadmap is collaboratively developed, defined, and produced by the whole community!

The Product Working Group is the core group of product managers and engineers who get together to drive the vision statement, major pieces of work, and perform coordination glue work. They also give feedback to community members interested in developing new features.

For more information about working groups, including how to join in on a meeting, see the Working Groups section of this course.
