The Open edX® project is a leading open-source learning software technology that powers courses on edx.org, on platforms for countries like Israel and France, and for organizations worldwide such as Microsoft and IBM. It is highly scalable, reliable, and customizable to meet any institution's online learning deliverables. It's stewarded by Axim Collaborative, a nonprofit organization.

Open source is an exciting part of the eLearning industry and the Open edX learning platform is one of the industry’s major players, making it quite the competitor. The platform's unique features and attributes make it very powerful and resourceful for eLearning platforms. Thanks to the availability of custom solutions, enterprise development, and multisites - as well as the integrated extension points of LTI Advantage and XBlocks - the platform provides its users with the opportunity to create personalized eLearning platforms with enormous flexibility. Today, more than 55 million learners use the Open edX software to develop in-demand skills and earn new credentials.
