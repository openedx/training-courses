In this module, we learned:

  • The Open edX® project is a leading open-source learning software technology that powers e-learning sites worldwide. This is a decentralized project which is moved forward by the Open edX Community, which includes all the organizations and people (universities, NPOs, providers, individuals, etc.) that use this technology.
  • We tackled the problem of our huge software product in an OEP called OEP-57 that sought to define the core product of our project. This defines a set of product guidelines for the Open edX platform, including a Product Narrative, Product Offerings, and Component Tiers, and describes how these principles impact the community.
  • The Open edX roadmap documents all current work in-flight and everything that's planned for the future across the Open edX ecosystem.

  • The roadmap (available to view on GitHub) is collaboratively developed, defined, and produced by the whole community... perhaps someday soon, by you, too!
