Monthly Meetup

The Open edX team hosts monthly meetups, featuring leading EdTech experts who share how to successfully integrate & leverage the Open edX platform to optimize learner outcomes. Presentations from community members and open time for discussion also provides a forum for connecting, collaborating, and networking with the vast and vibrant Open edX Community.

The monthly Open edX Meetups take place virtually in Gather.io and you can find the information, regarding upcoming Open edX Meetups, on our Event Calendar (Google calendar link) or by looking through our Eventbrite (ticketing) page. Events are free to attend, we just ask that you register on Eventbrite first.

Videos from previous meetups are all available on our YouTube page.

Meetups take place in Gather.io, a fun platform that gives you an avatar to walk around, meet people, and engage in small group discussions
