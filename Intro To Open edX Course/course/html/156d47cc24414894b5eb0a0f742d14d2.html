In this module, we learned:

  • As a community, we use different communications mediums: Discourse, Confluence wiki, GitHub, Slack or the docs.openedx.org documentation site. Each site is helpful for different situations.
  • There are several ways of contributing to the project, like being part of one of the Open edX working groups 
  • There is a monthly Open edX meetup, and a yearly conference
  • Another way of contributing is participating in the creation and testing of Named Releases
  • You can also become a Core Contributor or be part of the Maintainer program
