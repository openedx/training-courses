We get it - jumping in to a new community can feel intimidating. But we'd like you to know that we're so excited that you're here! Here's some ways that you can get going with the community:

  1. Try introducing yourself on the forums! Tell us about your skills and where you'd like to help out.
  2. Attend working group meetings - it's a great place to meet some people interested in areas that you're interested in, and put some faces to names
  3. Chat with people in working group Slack chat rooms - the active working groups page has a list of all active working groups and their Slack chat rooms

Everyone in the Open edX community follows the Open edX Code of Conduct in all interactions with one another. If you encounter interactions that don't adhere to the Code of Conduct, please reach out to the appropriate people as described in the Government & Enforcement section of that page.
