If you want to have an overall understanding of the Open edX project, and/or you want to contribute in the future, you're in the right place!

You might be interested in checking out the full range of capabilities the Open edX platform has. The Demo Course is full of various types of assessments that showcase some, but not all, of the things the Open edX software can do. The Open edX sandbox is a site where you can have a chance to play with both the authoring tool (Studio) and the learner experience. We invite you to check these out - we'll be here when you come back.

This course is meant as an onboarding to the Open edX project & community, so you won't need much context to take this course. For a deeper dive, we recommend you visit our Documentation site for more detailed documentation.

With that said... let's get going!
