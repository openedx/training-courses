For those of you interested in contributing code to the Open edX project, we've prepared a Developer Onboarding course to help you orient yourself with the coding community, our tech stack, our developer environment (called Tutor), and our coding conventions. In this section of the course, we'll give aspiring coders a brief overview of the Open edX architecture and an overview of contribution specifics, but we recommend you take the Developer Onboarding course for a more in-depth exploration of these topics.
