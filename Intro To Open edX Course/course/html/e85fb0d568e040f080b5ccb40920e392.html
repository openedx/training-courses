After finishing this course you'll:

  • Learn a bit about the project's history;
  • Know what we mean when we talk about the "Open edX community";
  • Understand how the community operates;
  • Learn about the different ways that people contribute;
  • And, you'll have enough context to start your contribution journey with the Open edX project!

We encourage you to take the next steps described at the end of the course so your first interactions with the project go as smoothly as possible. Good luck!
