What if we want a new theme? More services? Different settings? Or service behaviors? We’ve covered some of these use cases in the Making your Tutor environment developer friendly section, but let’s discuss Tutor customizations.

The local installation generated by Tutor can be customized using different strategies depending on what we need. Let’s list a few examples of what we might need to do:

      • Adding an external service that runs alongside your tutor installation
      • Changing docker containers behaviors
      • Adding new requirements to your docker images
      • Changing the Open edX services behavior like: changing hosts, platform name, contact email, passwords, etc
      • Changing the appearance of your installation
