Welcome to Module 1: The Open edX Platform: A Technical Overview

The Open edX platform is complex, utilizing many different technologies. We’ll walk you through the basic technologies we use, and then inspect the platform architecture at a high level. By the end of this module, you will be able to:

      • Identify the basics of our technical stack
      • Recognize the frontend and backend architecture.

Much of this unit contains topics that could be (and in some cases, are) their own courses. We provide links to many additional resources in Module 6, Reference Material: Tutorials & How-Tos.
