Join Us On Slack!

The Open edX Community uses Slack for real-time communication. This is good for coordinating meetings with working groups (discussed in the next section) or discussing with another community member how to split up a work item. For general questions, though, we encourage you to use Discourse. Slack's history is not as searchable as Discourse's history, and Slack history disappears after 6 months.
