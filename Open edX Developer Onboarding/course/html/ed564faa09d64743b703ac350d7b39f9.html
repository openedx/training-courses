Tutor is a community-supported Docker-based Open edX distribution both for production and local development. The goal of Tutor is to make it easy to deploy, customize, upgrade and scale an Open edX installation. Tutor is reliable, fast, extensible, and it is already being used to deploy hundreds of Open edX platforms around the world.

Tutor is actively maintained by Régis Behmo, the CTO of Edly. Régis has been working on Tutor since early 2018; he has been a contributor to the Open edX project since 2015. In particular, he worked for 2 years at FUN-MOOC, one of the top 5 largest Open edX platforms in the world. In addition, the Tutor project is a community-led project with many contributions from its project maintainers.

Documentation for development is available here: https://docs.tutor.overhang.io/dev.html
