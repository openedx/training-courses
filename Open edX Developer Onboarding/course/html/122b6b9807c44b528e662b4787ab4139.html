Fun fact!

One question you may or may not have is, how do we know to serve content in another language? In the Open edX software, the Account MFE allows users to set their preferred language to one that is supported by the site. In other Django sites, the browser’s accept header sends language information to the site, and special Django middleware intercepts the request, parses the header, and sends along locale information to the site. See this article on lokalise.com for more information.
