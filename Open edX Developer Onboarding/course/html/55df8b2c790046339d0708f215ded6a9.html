To Learn More:

As mentioned previously, edx.org/2U runs continuous deployment off master. This situation may change, but as of writing (2024), 2U/edx.org operates off master in most repositories. Occasionally, community contributed changes cause problems on the edx.org website. In this case, your change may be reverted and/or you may be asked to help out with a fix. Please pay attention to notifications on your pull request for at least 2 business days after it merges in case your PR causes any sort of issue.
